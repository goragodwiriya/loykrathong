About Loy Krathong

Loy Krathong is a beautiful Thai festival celebrating the water goddess Ganga. People create beautiful lotus-shaped floats (krathongs) adorned with flowers, candles, and incense sticks. By floating their krathongs on the water, people pay respects and make wishes for prosperity and happiness.
